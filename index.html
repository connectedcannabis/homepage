CULTIVATION ANALYTICS DASHBOARDS

California Yield Projections by Site

This chart uses the updated room yield goals and site processing times, to forecast Est. LTB for all of California to the end of 2026. Data is filterable by harvest date or FGI date and can be displayed in a weekly or monthly format.
Updated: 6/24/2025

Strain Level, Yield, QC, & THC data

This chart combines yield data and QC fail data from the Master Schedule with THC & terpenoid data from the QC response data. You can update dataset by site, by brand, and can recalculate based on your selected time period.
Updated: 6/24/2025

Forecasted Biomass Breakdown by Brand

This chart uses the actual and estimated yield from the master schedule to demonstrate brand allocation by Act./Est. lbs. Forecast is extended by using the output of planting mix to get the most forward looking forcast of production by brand
Updated: 6/24/2025

Clone Survival Rates

This chart displays clone survival rates for all strains currently on menu. Descriptive statistics are calculated from Bandwagon's cut record beginning in Jan. 2025. Strains are ranked from left to right based on their propagation performance. Filtering by brand is supported by the dropdown menu on the top left.
Updated: 6/30/2025

California QC Fails Dashboard

Tableau dashboard of all QC fails year-to-date based on the Master Schedule's Act. and Adj. lbs. Data is displayed overtime and is broken down by turn and strain for further analysis.
Updated: Daily
